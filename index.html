Privacy Policy for WallpaperX

Last updated: May 4, 2026

WallpaperX ("we", "our", or "us") is a free wallpaper application developed by Ntech. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly. However, our third-party advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID)
  • IP address and general location
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device type, OS version, and language

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data solely to display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob. We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any other third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

WallpaperX uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob – for serving advertisements
  • Cloudinary – for hosting and delivering wallpaper images

4. Permissions Used

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET – to load wallpapers and ads from the internet
  • SET_WALLPAPER – to set selected images as your device wallpaper
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – to save wallpapers to your gallery (Android 9 and below)

5. Children's Privacy

WallpaperX is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

6. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our own servers. Any data collected by AdMob is governed by Google's data retention policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
